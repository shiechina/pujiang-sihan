About Us

Pujiang Sihan Import and Export Co., Ltd.

Established in Pujiang County, Zhejiang Province, we are a professional manufacturer and exporter specializing in cleaning products and auto detailing tools. With years of experience in international trade, we have built a reputation for quality, reliability, and competitive pricing.

Our product range covers premium cotton mop heads for commercial and residential cleaning, as well as a comprehensive line of auto detailing tools including brushes, mitts, towels, and applicators. We serve clients across Europe, North America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.
